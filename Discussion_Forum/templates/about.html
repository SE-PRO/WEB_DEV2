About the site

Done under a course as project

A site where “anyone” can share their experience of being ill-treated because of their gender. This site was made in regard with one of the Sustainable Development Goals given by the UNO, Gender Equality.

The idea is to make the common users see the world from a victim’s perspective. From the victim's side, they can know what someone else who faced the same grief as them, did to gain strength. It helps a gender person to look at the problems faced by a person of the opposite gender.

This world generally sees people as weak and strong based on their gender. Men are considered to be strong and women are considered weak. But not all men are strong and not all women are weak. There are many incidents where women are taking advantage of laws and putting blame on men.

The discussion forum made, enables people to post their own forums and others can add their comments to any forum.

About the Logo and Name

The Logo was designed with just one thought- to free those who are meant to be free, the bird cage where humans pet their birds for their own delight, without understanding the plight of the bird and it's will to fly. We hope that people will break those shackles and fly high.

Also we named the site 2 Worlds, as there are 2 Worlds. One filled with utter darkness, where people are forced to become victims of something, against their will. Other world where people live with pride and joy, not caring about deeds. The colors chosen- black and blue, thus represent the 2 Worlds. We hope that the people in black world continue to pursue their dreams and fly high into the blue sky.
